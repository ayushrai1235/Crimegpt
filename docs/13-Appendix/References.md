## 2. Legal and Compliance Context
*Note: Developers should be familiar with the broad strokes of these laws as they relate to data privacy and digital evidence.*

- **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023:** Guidelines on handling citizen PII.
- **Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC) - Section 91:** Legal framework regarding the issuance of notices to produce documents (relevant for the DSS automated drafting feature).
- **Indian Evidence Act - Section 65B:** Admissibility of electronic records (relevant for ensuring audit logs are immutable).
